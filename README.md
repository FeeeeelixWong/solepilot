# SolePilot

SolePilot is a governed autonomy layer for one-person companies. It turns an
opportunity into an executable mission, lets specialist agents do the routine
work, and routes consequential actions through explicit owner policies.

## Why it exists

Solo founders can now delegate research, planning, outreach, and operations to
agents. The missing layer is control: who may contact a customer, commit to a
deadline, or spend money? SolePilot makes those boundaries visible and
verifiable instead of hiding them inside prompts.
